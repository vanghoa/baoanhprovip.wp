LED text under Singapore MRT

Mar 2021 — Apr 2021

A research on local text display technology on LED screen. This is a from the photography module during my year 2 at LASALLE College of the Arts

A school study of localized text display technology on LED screen as well as the font rasterization method developed throughout the history of SMRT trains in SG.

Various intriguing letterforms and typeface categories/treatments are documented.

  • Bảo Anh as photographer and graphic designer
  • Timothy Wee as instructor
  • Work done at LASALLE College of the Arts
