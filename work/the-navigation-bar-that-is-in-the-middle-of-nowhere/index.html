A website with its navbar in the middle?. During my time at Practice Theory, the school required me do a weekly vlog to record my internship experience there. I developed a whole website dedicated for this!
  • Visit the vanghoa.github.io/vlog-template/ to see the website.
  • Take a look at the github repository.

The concept of the “navigation bar in the middle of nowhere” had been on my checklist for a while. It could be seen as an additional meaning layer on top of the navigation bar surrounding the four sides on my previous ‘Personal website 2022’.

Initially, the website’s objective is to archive all of my video footages and images during my internship at Practice Theory. The idea was to use the site as a presentation platform for my vlogs, where I would screen record and narrate them using my voice. It was a convenient way to showcase my experiences and work on a weekly basis.

  • The website was done as part of an assignment at LASALLE College of the Arts
