Nov 2020 — Jan 2021

Website and illustrations for a magazine about the idea of Vietnamese art and the youth. I was part of this project during my time with the Vietnamme organization, probably in my year 2 of college, working alongside a team of designers and illustrators.

Vietnamme, an art organization in Vietnam, had been proposing the idea of a web magazine platform dedicated to exploring how the youth redefine and push the boundaries of art, irrespective of its form. I’m initially tasked with producing digital illustrations for two articles within the magazine.

  • Bảo Anh as website co-developer, website designer and illustrator
  • Diệu Ly as website co-developer and graphic designer
  • Minh Anh, and My Hà as graphic designer
  • Minh Phương, Anh Huy, and Chi Doan as illustrator
  • Vietnamme Magazine team for other content, and copy
