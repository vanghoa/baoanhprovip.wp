Love is Hard, HIV Prevention is Easy website

design

design

Sep 2021 — Nov 2021

Website for a public health campaign in Vietnam. This website is part of a bigger communication campaign by The Lab Saigon in 2021 – Love is Hard, HIV Prevention.

UI/UX design for a public health campaign website to raise awareness about HIV prevention, reduce new HIV infections, and eliminate HIV stigma in Vietnam–Love is Hard, HIV Prevention is Easy. The website acts as an online repository for the public audience to learn about the issues and the campaign itself.

Visit yeumoikho.com to see website.

  • Bảo Anh as UI/UX designer
  • Khê Nguyễn as UI/UX designer
  • Trang Đinh as UI/UX designer
  • Tuân Lê as Creative director
  • Digitop for web development
  • Work done as part of ‘LOVE IS HARD. HIV PREVENTION IS EASY.’ communication campagin by The Lab Saigon
